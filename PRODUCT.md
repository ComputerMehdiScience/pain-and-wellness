# Pain & Wellness Solutions

## Product Purpose
Marketing website for Kathy Morton, certified holistic therapist in Stirling, Ontario. Primary goal: drive new client bookings by earning trust quickly. Secondary goal: establish Kathy as the definitive local authority for Bowen therapy for humans, horses, and dogs in Hastings County.

## Register
brand

## Users

**Human wellness clients (primary):** Adults 35-65, rural/small-town Ontario (Hastings, Prince Edward, Northumberland counties). Frustrated with conventional medicine for chronic pain. Health-conscious but skeptical of "woo." They need to trust Kathy personally before they'll book. They search Google on their phone while sitting at their kitchen table.

**Horse owners / equestrians (high value):** Ages 30-60, rural landowners. Deeply invested in their horse's wellbeing. They trust practitioners who "live the life." Word-of-mouth dominant — one successful result spreads through barn networks fast.

**Dog owners / pet wellness seekers:** Ages 30-60. Treat pets as family. Already open to holistic care. Often cross-sell from human clients.

## Brand Tone
Warm, grounded, direct. Not "spa luxury." Not clinical. Not woo. Kathy is a neighbour who happens to be excellent at what she does. Rural Ontario authenticity. The copy should feel like someone who grew up with horses talking to someone who also grew up with horses.

## Anti-references
- The existing painandwellnesssolutions.ca (teal/white, butterfly clipart, cluttered, stock images)
- Generic wellness spa sites (white background, purple gradients, lotus flowers, stock photography of strangers)
- Clinical health sites (stark white, institutional blue, doctor stock photos)
- Squarespace/Wix wellness templates
- Any site where the colour palette is immediately guessable from the category

## Key Differentiator
Kathy is one of the only practitioners in the Quinte/Stirling region who combines human AND animal Bowen therapy under one brand. She grew up with horses (cattle sorting background) — she reads animals intuitively. This is not a practitioner who added an animal service as an upsell; it's genuinely who she is.

## Key Competitive Gaps to Address
- No FAQ page (leaving objections unanswered)
- Contact info buried (no hours visible)
- No insurance/payment info (major conversion barrier)
- Booking CTA not persistent
- Navigation overlap and confusion
- No privacy policy (affects Google trust)
